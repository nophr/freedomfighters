What is Freedom Fighter?

Freedom Fighter is a web application that helps you answer the following questions:



"What's the fastest route to where I want to go?""

"Would I have saved more money if I took the train instead of driving?"

"How much carbon have I contributed to the environment?"



Freedom Fighter empowers you by providing information
that could help you save time and the environment
